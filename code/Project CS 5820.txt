-- How to connect phone camera to computer

1. Connect phone through USB 
2. Go to Phone Settings --> Connected Devices --> USB Preferences, give access to Webcam
3. Go to Computer Settings --> Bluetooth & Devices --> Cameras, you should see that your phone's webcam is an option
